ABOUT

Main Sections

The "Temperature Around Us" project is a climate education website that uses data from NASA. Provides information about the temperature in different parts of the world and the factors that influence the climate.

Benefits

Learn about the climate in an easy and accessible way. Access reliable and updated information. Experience a visually appealing design. Available online from anywhere.

Goals

We hope to achieve the following with this project: Create an effective educational tool for understanding climate. Promote awareness about climate and its impact on the world. Inspire actions to protect our environment.
